## EPIC-04: Fraud Detection & Prevention

**Goal:** Automatically detect and block suspicious transactions in real time using a configurable rule-based engine, and provide Fraud Analysts with tools to review and resolve flagged cases.

**Scope:**
- Included: Fraud rule management (AMOUNT_EXCEEDS, TRANSACTION_FREQUENCY, UNUSUAL_HOUR), auto-block on rule match, customer notifications on block, analyst review dashboard, analyst unblock/reject workflow
- Excluded: ML-based detection, customer-initiated disputes, rule versioning/scheduling, bulk analyst review
