## EPIC-05: Platform Administration

**Goal:** Provide Bank Admins with tools to manage customer user accounts and maintain a tamper-proof audit trail of all significant system actions for compliance and incident investigation.

**Scope:**
- Included: Customer user management (activate/deactivate/unlock), system audit log with filtering
- Excluded: BI dashboards, reporting exports, SIEM integration, GDPR deletion requests, bulk operations

**Stories:** US-019, US-020
