## EPIC-02: Account Management

**Goal:** Allow customers to open and view Checking/Savings accounts, and give Bank Admins full oversight and control over the account portfolio.

**Scope:**
- Included: Open Checking/Savings accounts, view account list and details, admin account management (activate/deactivate)
- Excluded: Credit cards, loans, joint accounts, external accounts, initial deposit at account opening

**Stories:** US-006, US-007, US-008, US-009
